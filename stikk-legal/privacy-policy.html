Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026 · Last updated: June 22, 2026

Stikk ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application developed by milko labs that lets you convert images and videos into WhatsApp stickers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Summary: Stikk processes your images and videos entirely on your device. We only collect the data necessary to provide community features, authentication, and to keep the app running smoothly.

1.3 Information we DO NOT collect

  • Precise location: We do not access GPS or precise location data.
  • Contacts or call logs: Never accessed.
  • Images/videos processed locally: When you create stickers, all image processing (including AI-powered subject segmentation) happens 100% on your device using Google ML Kit. These images are never uploaded to our servers unless you choose to publish them to the community.
  • Microphone or camera recordings: Not captured or stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: Authentication, sticker creation, community features.
  • Improve the app: Crash reports and analytics help us fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Monetization: Display advertisements (free tier) and manage premium subscriptions.
  • Safety & moderation: Detect abuse, enforce rate limits, and respond to content reports.
  • Communication: In-app notifications about likes, downloads, and system updates.

3. AI and On-Device Processing

Stikk uses Google ML Kit Subject Segmentation to automatically separate subjects from backgrounds in your images. This AI processing:

  • Runs entirely on your device (on-device / edge processing).
  • Does not send your images to any cloud server for AI processing.
  • Does not train or improve any AI model with your images.
  • The ML Kit model is downloaded once during app installation and runs locally.

5. Data Sharing

  • With other users: Your username, avatar, and published stickers/packages are visible to the community.
  • With service providers: As described in Section 4 (Firebase, AdMob, Supabase, RevenueCat).
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • We do NOT sell your personal data.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Published stickers: Retained until you delete them or your account.
  • Crash logs: Retained by Firebase for up to 90 days.
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months.
  • After account deletion: All your data (profile, stickers, packages, likes, notifications) is permanently deleted from our servers. Third-party services may retain anonymized data according to their own policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, or applicable local law), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings → Delete Account) or by contacting us.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).
  • Withdraw consent: At any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at stikk.app@gmail.com or visit our Data Control page.

8. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS encryption.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies.
  • Server-side rate limiting prevents abuse.
  • Passwords and API keys are stored securely and never exposed to clients.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Stikk is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own (including the United States) where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required by GDPR.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Política de Privacidad

Fecha de vigencia: 22 de junio de 2026 · Última actualización: 22 de junio de 2026

Stikk ("nosotros", "nuestro" o "la App") es una aplicación móvil desarrollada por milko labs que te permite convertir imágenes y videos en stickers de WhatsApp. Esta Política de Privacidad explica qué datos recopilamos, cómo los usamos y tus derechos respecto a tu información personal.

Resumen: Stikk procesa tus imágenes y videos completamente en tu dispositivo. Solo recopilamos los datos necesarios para las funciones de comunidad, autenticación y mantener la app funcionando correctamente.

1.3 Información que NO recopilamos

  • Ubicación precisa: No accedemos a datos de GPS ni ubicación precisa.
  • Contactos ni registros de llamadas: Nunca accedidos.
  • Imágenes/videos procesados localmente: Al crear stickers, todo el procesamiento de imágenes (incluida la segmentación por IA) ocurre 100% en tu dispositivo usando Google ML Kit. Estas imágenes nunca se suben a nuestros servidores a menos que elijas publicarlas en la comunidad.
  • Grabaciones de micrófono o cámara: No se capturan ni almacenan.

2. Cómo Usamos tu Información

  • Proveer el servicio: Autenticación, creación de stickers, funciones de comunidad.
  • Mejorar la app: Los informes de fallos y analíticas nos ayudan a corregir errores y mejorar el rendimiento.
  • Monetización: Mostrar anuncios (nivel gratuito) y gestionar suscripciones premium.
  • Seguridad y moderación: Detectar abuso, aplicar límites de uso y responder a reportes de contenido.
  • Comunicación: Notificaciones dentro de la app sobre "me gusta", descargas y actualizaciones del sistema.

3. IA y Procesamiento en el Dispositivo

Stikk utiliza Google ML Kit Subject Segmentation para separar automáticamente sujetos del fondo en tus imágenes. Este procesamiento de IA:

  • Se ejecuta completamente en tu dispositivo (procesamiento local / edge).
  • No envía tus imágenes a ningún servidor en la nube para procesamiento de IA.
  • No entrena ni mejora ningún modelo de IA con tus imágenes.
  • El modelo de ML Kit se descarga una vez durante la instalación y se ejecuta localmente.

8. Seguridad de los Datos

  • El acceso a la base de datos está protegido por políticas de Row Level Security (RLS).
  • Limitación de tasa del lado del servidor previene abusos.
  • Las contraseñas y claves API se almacenan de forma segura y nunca se exponen a los clientes.

Aunque implementamos medidas de seguridad estándar de la industria, ningún método de transmisión electrónica es 100% seguro.

Stikk no está destinada a menores de 13 años. No recopilamos intencionalmente información personal de menores de 13 años. Si eres padre o tutor y crees que tu hijo/a nos ha proporcionado datos personales, contáctanos y los eliminaremos de inmediato.
